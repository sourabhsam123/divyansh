Expressing creativity and passion through music.

Flute

I have always had a penchant for music, especially playing flute. My journey with the Carnatic flute began at the age of five, and over the past decade, it has evolved into an integral part of my identity. Under the guidance of my guru, Shri Tiruchy Sarvanan, I have reached Grade 6 and performed at numerous venues across Singapore, from temples and schools to major cultural events. One of my most memorable performances was at GIIS in 2019, where I played before an audience of over 500 people. Winning the Papanasam Sivan Competition twice and competing at the national level have provided me with invaluable experiences, teaching me discipline, perseverance, and the importance of expressing emotion through music. More recently, I have taken my passion online, launching a YouTube channel where I share performances and explore new compositions, allowing me to connect with a wider audience. Below, I have included some of the prestigious competitions I had the opportunity to participate and even emerge victorious in.

Carnatic Flute Certifications:

  • Singapore Malayalee Hindu Samajam Singapore Yuvajanotsavam 2017 First Prize (Flute) 2017.
  • Sivan Fine Arts Academy Papanasam Sivan Songs Competition, Singapore 1st Prize 2019.
  • Sivan Fine Arts Academy Papanasam Sivan Songs Competition, Singapore Participation Prize 2021.
  • Sivan Fine Arts Academy Papanasam Sivan Songs Competition, Singapore 1st Prize 2025.
