Ted ED Club – Active speaker

Since Grade 9, I have been an active member of my school’s TED-Ed Club, where I have had the opportunity to refine my public speaking and communication skills. Over the years, I have delivered two TED Talks—one addressing the critical issue of mental health and another, more technical in nature, based on my research paper. These experiences have significantly enhanced my ability to structure speeches effectively and engage an audience with confidence.

Participating in the club has involved various activities, such as delivering impromptu speeches, engaging in peer feedback sessions, and ultimately presenting polished talks to a wider audience. Initially, the idea of standing in front of a large crowd felt intimidating, but with consistent practice and exposure, I have grown increasingly comfortable with public speaking. This journey has not only improved my confidence but has also strengthened my ability to articulate complex ideas clearly and persuasively.

Beyond my personal growth, I have also had the privilege of mentoring fellow students within the club. Whether it was helping them structure their speeches, refine their delivery, or overcome stage fright, I found great satisfaction in guiding others through the same challenges I once faced. Encouraging and supporting my peers has reinforced my belief in the power of effective communication and its ability to inspire change. Through my involvement in TED-Ed, I have not only developed a valuable skill set but have also contributed to fostering a culture of confident expression and meaningful dialogue within my school community.

Badminton Team

As a dedicated member of my school’s ACSIS A badminton team, I have been actively competing since Grade 6. Playing at a competitive level has not only allowed me to refine my technical skills, such as footwork, shot placement, and reflexes, but has also pushed me to develop mental resilience under pressure.

Beyond the physical and technical aspects, being part of the team has reinforced the importance of teamwork and sportsmanship. Encouraging my teammates, strategising doubles plays, and maintaining high morale during matches are all essential components of our success. The fast-paced rallies and intense matchups make every game a thrilling experience, requiring both endurance and adaptability. Through rigorous training and competitive play, I have grown both as an athlete and as an individual, learning valuable lessons in discipline, perseverance, and collaboration—qualities that extend far beyond the badminton court.

Maths Circle

From a young age, I have always challenged myself beyond the standard school curriculum, constantly looking for opportunities to explore mathematics in greater depth. Maths Circle has allowed me to do just that. In addition to learning about real-world applications of mathematics, I take an active role in leading discussions and working with fellow students to tackle such complex problems. The topics we explore extend far beyond IB mathematics, encouraging us to think outside the box, an approach similar to that when engaging in competitive Olympiads. I have been able to refine my problem-solving abilities and develop a more intuitive grasp of mathematical reasoning. More importantly, I enjoy fostering a collaborative learning environment where I guide my peers through intricate problems and deriving elegant solutions. My involvement in Maths Circle has not only deepened my passion for mathematics but also reinforced my ability to apply logical reasoning to further my mathematical ability along with those around me.
