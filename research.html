Fishtank Sensor System

The Coding for Good Service was my first introduction to programming, allowing me to both learn and apply newly developed skills in a meaningful way. In my first year, I focused on building a strong foundation in Python, Java, HTML, and CSS, ensuring that I could leverage this knowledge to contribute to projects that benefit my school and its associated communities. As I progressed, I sought opportunities to apply my coding expertise to real-world problems, leading me to work on a project that combined both software development and hardware integration.

In my second year, I developed a real-time temperature and pH monitoring system for the school’s fish tank. The system was designed to continuously measure and display live data and notify caretakers when the parameters deviated from the optimal range, ensuring a healthier aquatic environment.

The project utilized an Arduino microcontroller as the central processing unit, interfaced with temperature and pH sensors to capture environmental data. These sensors provided real-time readings, which were processed and displayed on an LCD screen for caretakers to monitor easily.

To enhance functionality, I integrated a Wi-Fi module, allowing the system to transmit live data to a web-based dashboard. This interface, built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabled remote monitoring of the fish tank’s conditions. The backend, developed in Python and Flask, handled data logging and generated alerts for caretakers.

This project combined both software and hardware elements, requiring me to apply my knowledge of circuit design, sensor calibration, and real-time data processing. Seeing the system in action—automatically detecting fluctuations and triggering responses— reinforced the practical impact of coding and embedded systems in real-world problem-solving. Through this experience, I not only deepened my understanding of programming and circuitry but also recognized how technology can be leveraged to create tangible improvements within a community.

Advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) model analyzing sentiment in tweets and forecasts stock market movements

I had the incredible opportunity to be part of the Inspirit AI program, developed by Stanford and MIT graduates, where I delved deep into the field of Machine Learning. This program provided a structured understanding of AI models, their accuracy, limitations, and real-world applications. More than just theory, it was a hands-on experience where I built models and implemented the concepts I had learned.

For my final project, I developed an advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) model that analyzes sentiment in tweets and forecasts stock market movements—predicting whether a stock should be bought, sold, or held based on social media sentiment. The process involved handling massive datasets, optimizing machine learning models, and validating predictions, strengthening my Python proficiency, data analysis skills, and model refinement abilities. More importantly, it deepened my understanding of AI’s intersection with finance, reinforcing my passion for leveraging technology to drive impactful change.

The Impact and Adoption of Artificial Intelligence in Finance and Economics : A Cross-Country Analysis

I authored a research paper titled The Impact and Adoption of Artificial Intelligence in Finance and Economics: A Cross-Country Analysis, with Dr. Mudasir Younis providing valuable guidance throughout the process. The paper delves into the evolving role of artificial intelligence within the financial and economic sectors, examining how AI is being adopted across different countries. It focuses on identifying the factors that influence its integration, as well as the challenges that countries face in leveraging this technology effectively.

The paper has been submitted for peer review, and Springer has accepted it for publication, with others currently reviewing it. The abstract offers a concise overview of the key discussions presented in the paper.

Through this research, I gained a deeper understanding of the research process itself, from data collection and analysis to the formulation of hypotheses and critical discussions. This experience allowed me to refine my analytical skills and better understand the intricacies involved in producing scholarly work.
