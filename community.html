FOUNDER - RO plant in Rajasthan School – India

During a visit to a local school in Pabana, Rajasthan, I witnessed firsthand the health risks that students and teachers faced due to a lack of access to clean drinking water. Recognising the urgent need for a solution, I decided to take initiative and personally fund the installation of a Reverse Osmosis Water Filtration Plant (Rs. 25,000).

To raise the necessary funds, I performed flute concerts at local events and gatherings, dedicating my earnings toward this cause. After months of saving, I was able to contribute the full amount needed to set up the filtration system, ensuring that 270 students and teachers could access safe drinking water every day. Seeing the students and teachers finally drink clean water was an incredibly fulfilling moment, reinforcing my belief that even small actions can create a lasting impact.

CHAIR PERSON - Electronic Outreach for Seniors

In Electronic Outreach with Seniors, we work with senior citizens and help them learn how to integrate electronics into fabrics and stitching. First and foremost, I have to go through sessions to help the student volunteers learn how to use the sewing machines and how to handle electronic equipment safely (as I am the chair). Then, when the senior citizens come by we introduce them to the volunteers who then help teach them how to operate the machines. We then transition into making a LED comfort emoji faced pillow using fabrics and a premade circuit allowing them to practically implement the skills they learnt.

After doing so we give them free choice to use their skills in different ways and create their own projects or create one of the suggested products which the student volunteers are fully trained in. Through this I have not only boosted my leadership and teaching skills but also my technical skills on how electronics work, sewing machines work and coming up with a baseline method to integrate both of them in practical everyday use objects which can be easily made by senior citizens. Each session is more than just a class—it’s a bridge between generations, fostering connections and mutual learning. Witnessing their excitement when they see their designs light up is a constant reminder of the joy of discovery, no matter one's age.

VOLUNTEER - Outreach for the Handicapped

Christian Outreach for the Handicapped is an institution which is a home for the handicapped who need a support system and COH provides them that. I got the wonderful opportunity to take care, interact and even teach COH residents where I was able to really understand how different life is for people with disabilities. It gave me a new perspective and made me respect them even more for persevering to achieve new heights despite the unfortunate circumstances they have to face.

FOUNDER KAWACH - Women safety app

KAWACH - Women Safety App is a groundbreaking mobile application designed to ensure the safety and well-being of women in all situations. With real-time location tracking, emergency alerts, and instant communication with trusted contacts, KAWACH provides a reliable and discreet way to seek help when needed. Whether you're traveling, commuting, or simply going about your day, KAWACH acts as your personal safety companion, giving you the confidence to face any challenges head-on. Empowering women to take control of their safety, KAWACH is a step towards a safer world for everyone.
